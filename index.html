Tandem

In 2018 a friend of mine opened a new cocktail bar in Newtown. It brought the warm, cosy feelings of Scandanavian bars and cafes to Sydney, embracing the concept of hygge.

This project was to stake a claim on the internet for this bar and allow for expanding upon later. The requirements were simply to get a landing page active on the domain that provided a contact email address.

We settled on a design that featured the logo, email address, street address and social media links. The website is responsive, accessible and fast to load. At the time of writing, this landing page has been replaced by the owner with a WordPress site.
